About

A Life Devoted to the Divine

🙏

Photo Coming Soon

Amar Vyas is a devoted Hindu priest with decades of experience performing sacred Vedic rituals and puja ceremonies across the United States.

For over 10 years, he served as a full-time pujari at the prestigious Shree Swaminarayan Mandir Vadtal, where he performed daily pujas for Shree Laxmi Narayan Dev and Shree Harikrushna Maharaj — one of the most revered spiritual responsibilities in the Swaminarayan tradition.

Based in Maryland, he travels across Delaware, Virginia, New Jersey, and throughout the United States to bring the blessings of authentic Vedic rituals to families and communities. He conducts ceremonies in English, Hindi, and Gujarati, making every family feel at home and connected to their roots.
